Mobility & Logistics

UNPRECEDENTED DATA COLLECTION & ANALYSIS FOR TRANSPORTATION

Objectives

Data collection and analysis for transportation to get the relationship between land use and economic activities and calculate the increase container/logistics volumes generated by the PIC growth.

Key Activities

• Collect data on three different scales across the corridor.

• Carry out stakeholder interviews and analyze current institutional and regulatory conditions.

• Develop regional origin-destination study.

• Develop multi-scenario model for future needs.

Deliverables

• Develop mobility plan with key indicators for the transportation and logistics sector.

• Proposals for 3 Mobility Projects at pre-investment level, including:

  • Multimodal transport hubs,
  • Non-motorized transport infrastructure,
  • Proposals for long-term transportation services for PIC workers.
