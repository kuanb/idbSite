Survey Plus: Data Collection Strategy

IMPLEMENTATION OF SOCIO-DEMOGRAPHIC & PUBLIC OPINION SURVEY

Note

Results pending completion of report. The below outlines present strategies for data acquisition and the state of completion for the report.

Objectives

Understand local conditions of each community to face the challenges the Rural Urban Continuum has the regional analysis through a “Survey Plus” implementation to collect basic socio-demographic data, information on risk preparedness and vulnerability as well as public opinion.

Key Aspects

• Random sampling in both urban and rural areas.

• 45 - 60 minute questionnaire per household, including modules on public opinion.

• Survey design by IDB specialists, risk assessment team, IDB specialists & local stakeholders through pilot.

• Sample and questionnaire design tailored to enable future economic impact assessment.

Status

• Fieldwork started in January of 2015.

• Tabulation to occur in April of 2015.
