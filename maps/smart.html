JOINT INITIATIVE PLANNING FOR GROWTH

Planning for Growth

To help Haitian communities address the challenges of Growth, ESCI has partnered with Haiti’s Interministerial Committee for Territorial Management and the Ministry of Economy and Finance.

Joint Objectives

• Mitigate short-term impacts while ensuring future sustainable growth.

• Generate and analyze previously unavailable data and information.

• Achieve the highest level of specificity in both diagnostic and future planning work.

• Translate a regional vision into site-specific and interdisciplinary plans for the communes closest to the PIC.

New Growth Dynamics

In addition to the Caracol Industrial Park, other anchor projects are currently being developed in the region:

  • Tourism
  • Seaport Infrastructure
  • Henri Cristophe University
  • New Housing Projects
  • Agriculture and Irrigation

Urban Challenges and Needs

Unplanned, rapid growth can increase conditions of informality as well as deteriorate community, landscape, and ecological conditions.
