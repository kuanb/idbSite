JOINT INITIATIVE PLANNING FOR GROWTH

Objective

Create a composite model based on diverse studies to identify key areas of the Region that need action by means of a development plan.

Key Aspects

An analysis of four categories of risk:

• Inland and coastal flooding.

• Seismicity. That is, the occurrence or frequency of earthquakes in a region.

• Hurricanes.

• Drought risks, including a water balance study.

Deliverables

• Probabilistic modeling for risk categories, including effects of climate change.

• Risk and exposure maps, including loss estimation for hazard events.

• Composite map that weights all the factors involved.

• Identification of attraction-restriction zones under two categories:

  • Highest aggregated risk zones.
  • Greatest expected risk resilience.
